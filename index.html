Enhancing Healthcare Security: Blockchain-Powered Solutions for Protecting Personal Health Records

Patient-Centric Access Control

Allows patients to have complete control over their healthcare data by granting or revoking access to doctors, hospitals, or researchers.

Blockchain-Based Secure Data Storage

Ensures that all Personal Healthcare Records (PHRs) are stored on a decentralized, tamper-proof blockchain network, preventing unauthorized access and data breaches.

Secure Medical Data Transmission & Encryption

Uses Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) and Secure Hash Algorithm (SHA) to encrypt and securely transmit medical records between patients, hospitals, and healthcare providers.

Disaster Recovery & Data Integrity Assurance

Ensures healthcare records remain accessible and untampered, even in cases of cyberattacks or system failures, by leveraging blockchain’s distributed ledger technology.

Securing Healthcare with Blockchain: Frequently Asked Questions.

What is the purpose of this project?

The project aims to protect Personal Healthcare Records (PHRs) from unauthorized access and data breaches using blockchain technology. By decentralizing data storage, it enhances security, privacy, and patient control over healthcare data.

How does blockchain improve healthcare data security?

Blockchain ensures data security through decentralization, immutability, and encryption. Unlike centralized databases, blockchain prevents unauthorized modifications, protects against cyberattacks, and allows only authorized users to access healthcare records.

What are the major challenges in the existing healthcare data management system?

The current system faces several challenges, including: Privacy risks due to centralized storage. Fragmentation of patient data across multiple providers. Limited control for patients over their health records. Vulnerability to cyberattacks and data breaches.

How does the proposed system address these challenges?

The proposed system integrates blockchain technology to: Provide a decentralized and secure healthcare data storage solution. Give patients complete control over who can access their records. Ensure data integrity through immutability and encryption. Enable secure and transparent access to medical records for authorized personnel.

What are the key modules of this system?

The system consists of the following key modules:Hospital,Cloud,Doctor,Patient,Intruder

What technologies are used in this project?

The project is developed using Full Stack Java with blockchain integration. Key technologies include: Blockchain for secure and decentralized data storage. Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) for encrypting healthcare records. Secure Hash Algorithm (SHA) for data integrity and verification. Smart Contracts to control data access.
